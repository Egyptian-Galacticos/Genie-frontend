Account Deactivation

Permanently deactivate your account and remove all data

What happens when you deactivate your account?

  • Your account will be temporarily suspended and you'll be logged out
  • Your profile and data will be hidden from other users
  • You won't be able to access features until reactivation
  • You can reactivate by logging in again within 30 days
  • After 30 days, your account and data will be permanently deleted

Easy reactivation within 30 days

  • Simply try to log in with your existing credentials
  • You'll be guided through a quick reactivation process
  • All your data and settings will be restored exactly as before
  • The 30-day window starts from the moment you deactivate

Consider these alternatives first

  • Update your profile information if something needs changing
  • Change your password if you have security concerns
  • Contact our support team if you're experiencing issues
  • Take a break - you don't need to deactivate to step away
